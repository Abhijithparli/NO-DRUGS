Our Mission

Fighting For A Drug-Free Kerala

We are committed to eliminating drug abuse through education, prevention, and community action.

"Say No To Drugs" is a state-wide initiative working in collaboration with schools, colleges, government agencies, and local communities to create awareness about the dangers of drug abuse and provide support to those affected.

Our comprehensive approach includes educational programs, counseling services, rehabilitation support, and community engagement activities to tackle the growing problem of drug abuse in Kerala.
